Who we are

Climate Quest is a community-led initiative focused on practical, local action. We design bite-sized challenges that reward environmentally sustainable practices and make climate learning a team effort.

How it works

  1. Gather your group and set a timer.
  2. Print the cards and score sheet.
  3. Watch the short intro video together.
  4. Draw a card and complete the challenge.
  5. Score points for teamwork and impact.

What you need

  • Printed deck + rules (PDF)
  • Score sheet (PDF)
  • Pens, phone for timing/photos

Watch the intro

Game rules (snapshot)

  • Play in rounds. Each round starts by drawing a card.
  • Agree on how you’ll complete the challenge safely.
  • Earn points for difficulty, teamwork, and real-world impact.
  • Bonus points for sharing proof or teaching others.
